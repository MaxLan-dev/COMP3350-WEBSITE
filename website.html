FitTrack

FitTrack empowers individuals to achieve their unique fitness and nutrition goals through a simple, adaptable exercise and weight management tool. Designed for busy, health‐conscious users, FitTrack consolidates your needs into one comprehensive system.

Features

Personalized Goals & Plans

Set personalized fitness goals—gain, maintain, or lose weight—and receive actionable guidelines. Modify your goals at any time with custom time frames.

Exercise Plans

Receive tailored exercise routines based on your unique goals.

Meal Suggestions

Enjoy meal plans aligned with your daily calorie needs and macronutrient details.

Custom Recipes

Add your own recipes to track and manage your dietary preferences.

Intended Users

FitTrack is crafted for anyone keen on tracking their calorie intake and exercise routines. Primarily, it serves health‐conscious individuals aged 15–30 who want to take an active role in managing their fitness and nutrition.
